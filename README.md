📊 Sales & Performance Dashboard
📌 Overview

This Power BI report provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance, profitability, and cost structure across different dimensions such as Brand, Company, Sales Channel (TT/MT), Region, and Time.

The dashboard is designed to support data-driven decision-making by highlighting trends, growth, and efficiency metrics.

🎯 Key Metrics

The report tracks the following KPIs:

Total Sales
Total Sales with Discount
Total Discount
Total Sold Quantity
Total Marketing Cost
% Margin
📊 Report Pages & Insights
1. 📈 Sales Overview
Monthly sales trend with MoM Growth %
Yearly comparison with YoY Growth
Cost structure:
COGS %
Marketing Cost %
Discount %
Margin %

👉 Purpose:
Understand performance trends and profitability drivers over time

2. 🏷️ Brand Performance
Sales and margin by Brand
Cost structure comparison across brands and years

👉 Purpose:
Identify:

Top-performing brands
High-margin vs low-margin brands

Sales breakdown by Company and Address
Includes:
Sales vs Previous Year
Quantity sold

YoY Growth

Deep-dive into client-level performance and efficiency

Performance by Sales Channel

This dashboard helps to:

Monitor sales performance and growth

Support strategic decisions in distribution business
